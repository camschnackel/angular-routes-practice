Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange is AMAZING. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe he is played by Benedict Cumberbatch, a phenominal actor. Doctor Steven Strange's first movie delves into his origin. He's a world class doctor who loses the ability to use his hands due to a car accident. He journeys to Kamer-Taj in search of healing, where he is recruited by modern day sorcerers who draw power from other dimensions of the multiverse to shape reality.

tl;dr, he's a spell-wielding badass that warps reality!
